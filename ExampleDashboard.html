User Guide

OTA Update Information

1. Partition Scheme: In Arduino IDE, please select:
Tools -> Partition Scheme -> Minimal SPIFFS. e.g. 1.9MB APP with OTA.

Ref: Espressif Partition Guide.

If "Minimal SPIFFS" is missing for your board, add a custom ota_19mb.csv and update boards.txt. This allocates 1.9MB for the App, ensuring OTA updates work with large modern binaries.

2. The right file: Upload YourVersion.ino.bin.

⚠️ DO NOT upload 'YourVersion.merged.bin' or 'YourVersion.bootloader.bin'

... they will be too large for OTA and will likely cause an 'UPDATE FAIL' message.

YouTube API Key

Enable 'YouTube Data API v3' at the Google Cloud Console. Make sure your key has no IP restrictions that might block the ESP32.

Channel ID

Your ID starts with 'UC...'. You can find it in your YouTube Advanced Settings or by clicking your profile icon > Settings > Advanced.

Scroll Cycle

The number of minutes between info scrolls. The display will show the Subscriber count primarily, then cycle through Location and Local Time based on this setting.

Sleep & Wake Hr

Uses 24-hour format (0-23). To extend the life of your 32x8 LED matrices (and save power!), the display will turn off during the Sleep Hour and resume at the Wake Hour.

AP Mode

If the device cannot connect to your Wi-Fi, it creates its own hotspot: 'OneCircuit-Config'.
Connect with your phone and browse to 192.168.4.1 to update settings.
